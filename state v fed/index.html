Model Legislation Dominance
ALEC introduced 2,900+ model bills between 2010-2018, with over 600 becoming law. ALEC model bills pass at rates nearly 5x higher than average congressional legislation.
Funding Shift to States
Democratic groups planned $175M for 2024 state legislative races. Republicans maintained structural advantages despite being outspent 4-to-1, controlling 57 of 98 partisan chambers.
Accountability Crisis
90% of voters cannot identify their state legislator. Full-time statehouse reporters declined 6% from 2014-2022, falling from 904 to 850 nationally.
Democratic Backsliding
Political scientist Jake Grumbach warns that nationalization of state politics creates "laboratories against democracy" where national groups use state authority to suppress votes and gerrymander districts.
Policy Laboratory Variation
Red and blue states implement increasingly distinct agendas in healthcare, reproductive rights, and climate. This creates "variable citizenship" where fundamental rights depend on state residence.
Single-Party Trifecta Efficiency
States with single-party control show exceptional productivity. Colorado achieved 73.5% passage rate with unified Democratic control, while Texas passed 35.5% of 9,120 introduced bills.
